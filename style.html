Expanded Palettes (Generated)

These palettes are generated based on color theory to complement the primary color. They are useful for illustrations, accents, or more complex UIs.

Analogous Palette

Colors that are next to each other on the color wheel. Creates a serene and comfortable design.

Triadic Palette

Colors that are evenly spaced around the color wheel. Creates a vibrant, high-contrast effect.

Split-Complementary Palette

A variation of the complementary color scheme. In addition to the base color, it uses the two colors adjacent to its complement. This color scheme has the same strong visual contrast as the complementary color scheme, but has less tension.
