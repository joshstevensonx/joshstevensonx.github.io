Our Solutions

Providing a full spectrum of specialized services for the energy and marine sectors.

Construction & Fabrication

End-to-end services for civil and mechanical construction, structural steel fabrication, and pipeline installation. We ensure projects are completed to the highest standards of quality and safety.

Corrosion Control

Advanced asset protection through industrial sandblasting, painting, and specialized coating applications. We extend the life of critical infrastructure and prevent costly wear and tear.

Marine Logistics

Comprehensive marine support, including dredging, reclamation, and leasing of heavy-duty equipment like barges, tugboats, and crew vessels to support offshore operations.

Procurement & Supply

Strategic sourcing and reliable supply of industrial equipment, materials, spare parts, and personal protective equipment (PPE) to ensure your operations run smoothly.

Manpower Supply

Providing skilled, certified, and experienced technical manpower for all phases of onshore and offshore projects, ensuring you have the right expertise when you need it.

Inspection & Testing

Ensuring asset integrity and compliance with Non-Destructive Testing (NDT) services, pressure testing, and certification to meet international safety and quality standards.
