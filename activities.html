  • TERBADORE COFFEE BAR

    Situated at 87 Currys Post Road, Howick, 3290. This Coffee Bar offers intriguing coffee and pastries menu and majestic backdrops.

  • BATTLE FIELDS PUB & GRUB

    Battle Fields is a local bar/Resturant only 6kms from Bush Creek. As you hit the tar road (R103) Battlefields is situated opposite the tar road. This Pub and Grub often have great specials. They only open certain days of the week. Please contact 0767441754 for more information.

  • MOOI RIVER COUNTRY CLUB

    Mooi River Country Club is a 20 minute drive from Bush Creek. It is situated in the town of Mooi River. The resturant and bar also has the most amazing specials. Please contact 0826090860 to find out more.

  • MIDLANDS FABLES EATERY

    This devine Resturant and Cocktail bar is a 40 min drive from Bush Creek. It has a breath taking gardens to walk through. It is situated at 85 Curry's Post Road, Howick, KwaZulu-Natal, South Africa.

Local Activites

  • GOLF AT MOOI RIVER COUNTRY CLUB

    This scenic course is nestled amongst farmlands and mountain ranges. There is a bar, resturant and kids play groun. The Mooi River Golf Course has 11 holes with 18 tee boxes and a par 72, with a 71 rating.

  • WAGON DRIFT DAM AND RESERVE

    Wagendrift Dam and Moor Park Nature Reserve is situated just off the N3 highway near Estcourt. The reserve is roughly 980 ha in extent and includes a popular picnic and fishing spot.

  • NOTTINGHAM ROAD PARK RUN

    It is a 5km run or walk starting at 8am. The event takes places at the Windmills Ultrastop, N3 Nottingham Road, Curries Post Interchange, 3280. Experience a beautiful run/walk in the Blue Crane Nature Reserve.

  • EPIC CARTING, MIDLANDS

    This amazing venue has an outdoor gokart, target Shooting, and Sandart. There is also a Lifestyle Gifting shop and a Coffee Cafe (serving snacks, light lunches, milkshakes and cappuccinos).

Scenic Destinations

  • Weenen Nature Reserve

    The reserve offers extensive game-viewing facilities, guided walks, environmental education, and three picnic sites which makes it ideal for a day visit.

  • Piggly Wiggly Country Village

    It features a variety of family run-eateries and artisanal shops. You can explore unique local stores offerring hand crafted goods, enjoy gourmey treats and experience a range of activities for both kids and adults.

  • Nelson Mandela Capture Site

    Nelson Mandela was arrested on 05 August 1962 outside Howick, KZN. The Capture site commemorates this moment in history with a Musean and a world renowned Sculpture. Coffee and Gifts shops on site.
