About CEMEX

Our Company Profile

CEMEX PROJECT INTEGRATED LIMITED is a Nigerian company that provides a variety of services, primarily in the oil and gas and marine sectors. We stand on a foundation of technical expertise, innovation, and a deep-rooted commitment to delivering projects that exceed expectations.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide quality services to the oil & gas and marine sectors of the Nigerian economy, with a commitment to Health, Safety & Environment.

Our Vision

To be a reference point in the provision of quality, efficient and client-satisfaction-oriented services in Nigeria.

Health, Safety & Environment

Our HSE Policy is the cornerstone of our operations. We are committed to protecting the health and safety of our employees, minimizing our environmental impact, and adhering to all relevant HSE laws.
