Our Company Profile

CEMEX PROJECT INTEGRATED LIMITED is a wholly Nigerian company focused on providing integrated services to the oil and gas and marine sectors. Our expertise lies in delivering professional, cost-effective solutions tailored to the specific needs of our clients.

We stand on a foundation of technical expertise, innovation, and a deep-rooted commitment to delivering projects that exceed expectations.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality services to our clients safely and efficiently by utilizing our local technical expertise, with a strict adherence to our HSE policy, while promoting host community development.

Our Vision

To become the undisputed reference point for quality, efficiency, and client satisfaction in the Nigerian oil, gas, and marine industries.

Health, Safety & Environment

Our HSE Policy is not just a document; it's the cornerstone of our operations. We are fundamentally committed to an incident-free workplace and the protection of our environment.
