About CEMEX

Learn about our commitment to excellence, our guiding principles, and our unwavering dedication to safety and quality.

Our Company Profile

CEMEX Project Integrated LTD is a full-spectrum infrastructure company based in Lagos, Nigeria, with core expertise in Power Systems, Marine Services, Construction, and Facility Management.

We deliver reliable energy solutions, offshore and coastal marine support, integrated design-build services, and comprehensive facility management services. Our multidisciplinary approach ensures seamless execution: from powering critical operations to creating and maintaining environments that stand the test of time.

Established in 2015, we began as a bold, forward-thinking player in Nigeria's Power, Marine, and Construction sectors. Over the years, we have navigated complex industry challenges through strategic leadership, technical expertise, and a steadfast commitment to quality delivery. These strengths have enabled us to deliver impactful projects and evolve into a trusted name in the industry. Our journey is one of continuous transformation, driven by purpose, sustained by expertise, and measured by results we are proud to uphold.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To deliver reliable power, marine, construction and facility management solutions that drive sustainable growth, strengthen communities and support critical operations. We are committed to excellence, innovation and efficiency, ensuring every project we undertake creates long term value for our clients and stakeholders.

Our Vision

To set a new standard of infrastructure excellence by delivering sustainable power solutions, advancing marine services, and developing iconic projects that energize communities and drive progress across Nigeria and beyond.

Health, Safety & Environment

Our HSE Policy is the cornerstone of our operations. We are committed to protecting the health and safety of our employees, minimizing our environmental impact, and adhering to all relevant HSE laws.
