About CEMEX

Excellence in Oil, Gas & Marine Engineering

Who We Are

Powering Your Growth

CEMEX PROJECT INTEGRATED LIMITED is a wholly Nigerian company focused on providing integrated services to the oil and gas and marine sectors. Our expertise lies in delivering professional, cost-effective solutions tailored to the specific needs of our clients, from engineering and procurement to construction and marine logistics.

Our Compass

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide the highest quality services to our clients safely and efficiently by utilizing our local technical expertise, with a strict adherence to our Health, Safety & Environment (HSE) policy, while promoting the development of our host communities.

Our Vision

To become the undisputed reference point for quality, efficiency, and client satisfaction in the Nigerian oil, gas, and marine industries.

Our Pledge

HSE & Quality Policy

At CEMEX, our commitment to Health, Safety, and Environment (HSE) is integral to our business. We strive to exceed expectations by ensuring every project is delivered with the highest standards of safety and quality, protecting our people and the planet.

  • Protecting the health and safety of all employees and affected persons.
  • Minimizing the environmental impact of our activities.
  • Adhering to all relevant HSE laws and international standards.
  • Continuously improving our performance through training and review.
