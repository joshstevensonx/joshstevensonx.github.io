Locate Us

For better directions please read the below information:
  • You can type in “Battlefields Pub and Grub” into Google Maps.
  • This will take you to a restaurant on the R103 in between Mooi River and Estcourt.
  • This restaurant is directly opposite the dirt road you need to take to get to Bush Creek Guest House.
  • If you are coming from Estcourt / Midway N3 you will turn left onto the Dirt Road.
  • If you are coming from Mooi River / Durban area you will turn right onto the Dirt Road. There will be signs.
  • You need to drive 5,7kms on this dirt road. Keep on this dirt road do not via off.
  • Please keep following the Bush Creek Signs all the way to Bush Creek Guest House Black electric gate.

About Us

Bush Creek Guest House invites you to escape the hustle of city life. Nestled on a picturesque farm between Mooi River and Estcourt, our cozy bed and breakfast features six uniquely accommodating rooms. Enjoy free WiFi and on-site parking while taking in stunning rural views. Dive into our refreshing dam, embark on scenic river walks, or simply relax in the tranquility of nature. Discover a perfect blend of history, comfort, and adventure at Bush Creek Guest House, where your peaceful retreat awaits..

Tucked away in the scenic landscapes of KwaZulu-Natal, Mooi River is a destination that blends adventure, relaxation, and natural beauty. Whether you're an avid hiker, a nature enthusiast, or simply looking for a peaceful escape, this charming region has something to offer. At the heart of it all, Bush Creek Guest House provides the perfect base to explore rolling hills, lush valleys, and the soothing sounds of nearby rivers.

From dramatic mountain backdrops to hidden waterfalls, Mooi River is brimming with natural wonders waiting to be discovered. Outdoor enthusiasts can embark on exhilarating hikes through the Drakensberg Mountains, paddle along the Mooi River, or visit the awe-inspiring Tugela Falls. For those seeking a more relaxed experience, Hashtagg Farm and local nature reserves provide peaceful walks, birdwatching opportunities, and stunning panoramic views. Each attraction showcases the area’s rich biodiversity and invites visitors to connect with nature in a meaningful way.

Beyond its natural beauty, Mooi River charms with its welcoming atmosphere and local culture. Scenic trails lead to quiet spots perfect for reflection, while nearby markets offer handmade crafts and fresh produce. Whether you’re exploring wildlife reserves, uncovering hidden gems, or simply unwinding at Bush Creek Guest House, the region provides the perfect balance of adventure and tranquility. Let Mooi River’s enchanting landscapes and warm hospitality rejuvenate your spirit and inspire your next getaway.
